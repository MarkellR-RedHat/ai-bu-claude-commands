## Overview
A brief summary of what the competitor offers. Stick to facts. Include the product category, target audience, and deployment model (SaaS, self-hosted, hybrid, etc.) if known.

## What They Do Well
A bullet list of genuine strengths. Be honest. Ignoring a competitor's real advantages makes the analysis useless. Focus on:

- Developer experience
- Community and ecosystem
- Pricing model
- Documentation and onboarding

## Where They Fall Short
A bullet list of weaknesses, gaps, or common complaints. Pull from public sources like GitHub issues, community forums, and published reviews. Do not make things up. If you are uncertain about a claim, say so.

## Key Takeaways
Three to five bullet points summarizing what the team should know. Focus on actionable insights, not opinions.
